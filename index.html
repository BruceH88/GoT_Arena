Game of Thrones Arena

Instructions

  • When the game starts, click on the character you want to play.
  • The rest of the characters will become your opponents whom you must defeat to win.
  • Select the first opponent to fight.
  • Click the Attach button to attack the selected opponent and damage their health.
  • They will immediately counter attack you and damage your health.
  • When you bring an opponent's health to or below zero they are defeated and then you pick another opponent.
  • You win by defeating all the opponents and lose if your health drops to zero.
  • Each character has a different attack, counter attack, and health.
  • Each time you attach, your character's attack value will increase by its base value.
